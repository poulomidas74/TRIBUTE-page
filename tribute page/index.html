Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man & President Of India

What is APJ Abdul Kalam knows for?

Kalam served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
He was widely popular and admired as the "People's President" for his approachable demeanor and commitment to youth and education.

What organizations APJ Abdul Kalm associated with?


1.Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)
2. Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
3.Indian Institute of Management (IIMs)
4.Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU)
5.Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR)
